INVO - Empowering Offline Businesses establishing their online presence

It offers AI-powered billing, product galleries, price comparison tools, and a business interface for tracking both offline and online sales. Targeting small and medium-sized businesses, INVO simplifies business processes, improves market reach, and enhances operational efficiency, helping businesses thrive in a competitive landscape.

JustIQ - Legal awareness platform with interactive quizzes and games

 JustIQ is a web-based platform designed to raise legal awareness among the general public through engaging, interactive games and quizzes. It offers scenario-based challenges that test users' knowledge of laws, with multiple levels and progress tracking to encourage learning. The platform also provides simplified educational content on legal topics, helping users understand laws in a practical, accessible way. By making legal knowledge more approachable, JustIQ aims to empower individuals with the information they need to navigate everyday legal situations.

Vehicle Parking System using Python and Tkinter (GUI)

 The Vehicle Parking System is a Python application with a Tkinter-based GUI designed to streamline parking management. It generates unique tokens associated with the vehicle number, representing an available parking spot. Upon entering a vehicle number, the system assigns a token for a vacant space, and if the same vehicle number is entered again, it retrieves the original token, ensuring the vehicle can be easily located and retrieved. This system simplifies the parking process, making it more efficient and organized for users.

Digital Calculator using Java and JavaFX

 The Basic Digital Calculator is a simple Java application created using JavaFX that performs fundamental arithmetic operations. It includes core functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. With a minimalistic interface, this basic version is designed for straightforward, everyday calculations, offering ease of use and quick results.
